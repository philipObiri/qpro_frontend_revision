About Our Qualification Programs

Overview

"Qualifications for Professionals (QPro), a distinguished division of the Accra Business School, is dedicated to addressing the evolving training needs of 21st-century business professionals. Our focus is on delivering world-class, graduate-level, short-term training programs. These programs are specially tailored for individuals transitioning to new career areas without formal training and for emerging professionals eager to make a substantial impact in their workplaces. We strive to equip our participants with advanced skills and knowledge, ensuring they are well-prepared to meet the challenges and opportunities of today's dynamic business environment."

Mission Statement


"Our mission at Qualifications for Professionals is to empower business professionals through exceptional education. We commit to providing innovative and practical training solutions that bridge the gap between academia and the realities of the business world. By fostering a culture of continuous learning and professional growth, we aim to enhance the capabilities of individuals, thereby contributing to the advancement and success of modern organizations."

Vision Statement


"Our vision is to be a leading global center of excellence in professional training, recognized for transforming the careers of business professionals. We aspire to set the standard in short-term, graduate-level training, cultivating a community of forward-thinking, highly skilled professionals who drive positive change in their industries. Our ultimate goal is to influence the development of human capital on a global scale, making a lasting impact on the workforce of the future."
