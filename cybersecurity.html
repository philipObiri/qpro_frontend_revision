The Professional Development Certificate in Cybersecurity is an 8-week intensive online program designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of cybersecurity. The course covers a wide range of topics including risk management, security architecture, cryptography, and network security. This program is specifically tailored to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to protect computer systems and networks from various cyber threats.

Aim:
  • To prepare students with the essential knowledge and skills required to defend computer systems and networks against cyber threats.

Objectives:
  • Understand the fundamentals of cybersecurity, including risk management and security architecture.
  • Learn the principles of secure system design and implementation.
  • Identify and mitigate various security threats.
  • Develop skills to analyze and respond effectively to security incidents.

Upon completion of the course, participants will be able to:

  • Assess and identify potential security risks.
  • Design and implement secure systems.
  • Respond effectively to security incidents.
  • Develop strategies to protect systems and networks from cyber attacks.

Course Outline

Week 1: Foundations of Cybersecurity
  • Basics of cybersecurity, understanding cyber threats, risk management, and current trends in the field.

Week 2: Risk Management in Cybersecurity
  • In-depth analysis of risk assessment, legal frameworks, and strategic development for managing cybersecurity risks.

Week 3:Security Architecture
  • Principles of security architecture, threat modeling, risk assessment, and aspects of network security.

Week 4: Cryptography
  • Study of cryptographic principles, their application in data security, and understanding their limitations.

Week 5: Network Security
  • Exploration of network security protocols, firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and VPNs.

Week 6: Secure System Design and Implementation
  • Focus on secure coding practices, software security testing, and designing robust security architectures.

Week 7: Security Incident Analysis and Response
  • Techniques for incident detection, analysis, response planning, execution, and post-incident recovery.

Week 8: Capstone Project
  • A comprehensive project that encompasses all aspects of cybersecurity, applying the concepts and skills learned throughout the course.
