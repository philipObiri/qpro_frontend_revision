Professional Development Certificate in Human Resource Management

The Eight-Week Professional Development Certificate in Human Resource Management is an accelerated program designed to provide a deep dive into HR management. Covering essential areas such as recruitment, training, performance management, and employee relations, this course is ideal for those looking to excel in HR management within a fast-paced corporate environment.

Aim:
  • To quickly equip students with essential skills and knowledge for effective human resource management in diverse organizational contexts.

Objectives:
  • Provide a foundational understanding of HR management principles and practices.
  • Cover legal and ethical considerations relevant to HR.
  • Develop competencies in recruitment, training, and performance management.
  • Enhance understanding of employee relations and the roles of HR managers

Upon completion of the program, students will:

  • Have a clear understanding of key HR management concepts.
  • Recognize legal and ethical aspects of HR practices.
  • Manage recruitment, training, and performance evaluation processes effectively.
  • Navigate employee relations and understand HR managerial responsibilities.

Course Outline

Weeks 1 - Week 2: Introduction to Human Resource Management
  • Understanding HRM fundamentals and their strategic importance.
  • Legal and ethical frameworks governing HR practices.
  • Legal and ethical frameworks governing HR practices.

Weeks 3 - Week 4: Recruitment and Selection
  • Exploring modern recruitment strategies and selection methods.
  • Understanding the legal and ethical dimensions in recruitment.

Weeks 5 - Week 6: Training and Development
  • Designing and executing effective training programs.
  • Assessing the impact of training on employee development.

Weeks 7 - Week 8: Performance Management and Employee Relations
  • Strategies for assessing and enhancing employee performance.
  • Managing employee relations, including conflict resolution and workplace safety.

START DATE: 9th March 2024
FEES: 2500 GHS

This comprehensive 8-week program is perfectly suited for professionals aiming to rapidly enhance their HR skills and knowledge. It combines vital theoretical insights with practical application, preparing students for immediate and effective roles in various HR capacities within modern organizations.
