Professional Development Certificate in Operations Management

Introduction: The 8-week Professional Development Certificate in Operations Management is an intensive program offered by Qualifications for Professionals at Accra Business School. This course is specifically tailored for individuals aiming to excel in operations management across various business sectors. It encompasses essential aspects of operations management including planning, inventory control, quality management, supply chain management, and project management. The program is structured to deliver a practical and interactive learning experience, ensuring quick and effective skill acquisition.

Aims
  • To prepare participants for significant roles in operations management by providing crucial knowledge and skills.
  • To ensure participants gain a comprehensive understanding of operations management principles applicable in diverse industries

Objectives
  • Basics of Business Administration and Strategic Management.
  • Introduction to strategic analysis tools like SWOT, PESTEL, and Porter’s Five Forces.
  • Case study discussions and practical application.

Participants will achieve the following upon completing the 8-week course:

  • Broad Business Knowledge: Acquire a comprehensive understanding of major business disciplines and the ability to integrate this knowledge in various business contexts.
  • Strategic and Analytical Thinking: Enhance the ability to apply strategic and analytical thought processes to solve complex business problems.
  • Operational Competence: Gain skills in key areas of business operations, such as finance, marketing, and operations management.
  • Ethical and Global Awareness: Understand ethical business practices and the complexities of global business operations.
  • Entrepreneurial and Innovative Mindset: Develop entrepreneurial and innovative thinking, crucial for business growth and adaptability in changing markets

Program Structure

Week 1: Strategic Management and Business Foundations
  • Basics of Business Administration and Strategic Management.
  • Introduction to strategic analysis tools like SWOT, PESTEL, and Porter’s Five Forces.
  • Case study discussions and practical application.

Week 2: Financial Management Essentials
  • Fundamental concepts in finance, accounting, and budgeting.
  • Analysis of financial statements and investment decision-making.
  • Workshop on financial planning and forecasting.

Week 3: Marketing Management Strategies
  • Core marketing principles in contemporary business.
  • Exploration of digital marketing trends, branding, and consumer behavior.
  • Hands-on exercises in marketing planning.

Week 4: Operations Management for Efficiency
  • Key principles of process analysis, capacity planning, and lean management.
  • Techniques for quality control and supply chain optimization.
  • Case study analysis in operations management.

Week 5:: Organizational Behavior and Leadership
  • Analysis of individual and group dynamics within organizations.
  • Examination of various leadership styles and motivation theories.
  • Sessions on team building, conflict resolution, and change management.

Week 6: Business Ethics and Corporate Responsibility
  • Discussion on ethical theories, corporate governance, and corporate social responsibility.
  • Exploration of sustainability and ethical leadership.
  • Case studies focusing on ethical decision-making.

Week 7: Global Business Environment
  • Theories and practices in international business and global market analysis.
  • Understanding cross-cultural management and global strategy development.
  • Discussions on the impacts of globalization.

Week 8: Entrepreneurship and Innovation
  • Techniques for identifying business opportunities and ideation.
  • Insights into business model innovation and market strategies.
  • Final entrepreneurship project presentation and feedback session.

  • Emphasis on case studies and real-world applications.
