Professional Development Certificate in Operations Management

Introduction: The 8-week Professional Development Certificate in Operations Management is an intensive program offered by Qualifications for Professionals at Accra Business School. This course is specifically tailored for individuals aiming to excel in operations management across various business sectors. It encompasses essential aspects of operations management including planning, inventory control, quality management, supply chain management, and project management. The program is structured to deliver a practical and interactive learning experience, ensuring quick and effective skill acquisition.

Aims
  • To prepare participants for significant roles in operations management by providing crucial knowledge and skills.
  • To ensure participants gain a comprehensive understanding of operations management principles applicable in diverse industries

Objectives
  • Offer an extensive overview of operations management concepts and practices.
  • Provide insights into key areas such as operations planning, scheduling, and supply chain management.
  • Develop analytical skills for evaluating operations management processes.
  • Empower participants to utilize operations management concepts for practical problem-solving and strategic decision-making.

Upon completion of the program, participants will::

  • Have a solid understanding of operations management principles and practices.
  • Possess skills to analyze and optimize operations management processes.
  • Be able to apply operations management principles in real-life situations.
  • Be capable of devising strategies to improve operations management practices.
  • Entrepreneurial and Innovative Mindset: Develop entrepreneurial and innovative thinking, crucial for business growth and adaptability in changing markets

Program Structure

Week 1: Fundamentals of Operations Management
  • Introduction to key concepts and the scope of operations management.
  • Operations processes and strategies in both manufacturing and service industries.

Week 2: Operations Planning and Scheduling
  • Techniques in planning, demand forecasting, and capacity planning
  • Tools for scheduling including Gantt charts and PERT/CPM.

Week 3: Inventory Management
  • Systems for inventory control, Economic Order Quantity (EOQ), Just-In-Time (JIT) inventory.
  • The role of inventory management in supply chain performance.

Week 4: Quality Management
  • Total Quality Management (TQM), Six Sigma, ISO standards.
  • Processes for continuous improvement.

Week 5: Supply Chain Management
  • Supply chain design and strategy, logistics, and transportation management.
  • The role of technology, global strategies, and sustainability in SCM.

Week 6: Essentials of Project Management
  • Overview of the project lifecycle, planning, and scheduling tools.
  • Leadership and stakeholder management in project contexts.

Week 7: Contemporary Topics in Operations Management
  • Lean operations, digital transformation, and current industry trends.
  • Case studies and practical applications in operations management.

Week 8:Capstone Project and Strategy Formulation
  • A practical project incorporating learned concepts.
  • Developing and implementing strategies in operations management.
START DATE: 9th March 2024
FEES: 2500 GHS

This accelerated program is designed for professionals and students eager to develop their expertise in operations management. It equips them with the skills necessary to address operational challenges and seize opportunities in the ever-evolving business landscape.
