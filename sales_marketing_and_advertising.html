Professional Development Certificate in Marketing, Sales and Advertising.

Apply Now

  • Aims And Objectives
  • Learning Outcomes
  • Programme Structure
  • Fees And Start Date

The 8-week Professional Development Certificate in Marketing, Sales and Advertising is an intensive online program designed to offer comprehensive training in the interconnected fields of sales, marketing, and advertising. This course aims to provide students with a deep understanding of sales techniques, marketing strategies, and effective advertising practices. It is tailored for professionals seeking to enhance their skills in these dynamic and essential business areas.

Aims:
  • To equip participants with advanced knowledge and skills in sales, marketing, and advertising.
  • To prepare individuals for success in driving business growth through effective sales and marketing strategies.

Objectives:
  • Develop a thorough understanding of sales processes and techniques.
  • Explore various marketing strategies and their application in different business contexts.
  • Learn the fundamentals and creative aspects of advertising.
  • Integrate sales, marketing, and advertising concepts for comprehensive business development.

Upon completion of the program, participants will be able to:
  • Apply effective sales techniques and understand the sales cycle.
  • Formulate and implement innovative marketing strategies.
  • Design and execute impactful advertising campaigns.
  • Integrate sales, marketing, and advertising knowledge to drive business success.

Course Outline

Week 1 - Week 2: Foundations of Sales
  • Understanding the sales process, relationship building, and sales techniques.

Week 3 - Week4: Marketing Strategies
  • Exploring different marketing frameworks, digital marketing, and market analysis.

Week 5 - Week 6: Advertising Principles and Practices
  • Basics of advertising, creative strategy development, and media planning.

Week 7 - Week 8: Integrating Sales, Marketing, and Advertising
  • Strategies for aligning sales, marketing, and advertising efforts for optimal business performance.

Assessment and Certification:
  • The course will include practical assignments, project work, and case studies.
  • Upon successful completion, participants will receive a Professional Development Certificate in Sales, Marketing, and Advertising.

START DATE: 9th March 2024
FEES: 2500 GHS

This program is perfect for professionals aiming to expand their expertise in sales, marketing, and advertising. It offers a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, ensuring participants are well-prepared to contribute to business growth and development in these vital areas.
